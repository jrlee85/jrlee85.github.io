Profile

I’m James, a recent graduate of the University of London’s MSc in Data Science, with a strong command of data analysis and machine learning techniques. I achieved a distinction in my MSc, with an overall mark of 88% (4.0 GPA). I focused my Final Research Project on the area of Computer Vision, specifically classification of wildlife from camera trap images using convolutional neural networks. Since completing my MSc I have continued to work on a number of data science projects in order to increase my knowledge.

I possess 15 years’ prior professional experience in business, coupled with a diverse academic background in mathematics, law and philosophy. During my career I earned successive promotions due to strong performance and organizational impact. This has given me a broad skill set including outstanding research and analysis capabilities, excellent communication skills, and a strong commercial awareness.

Outside of work I enjoy playing the guitar and keeping active through football, running, cycling and climbing.
